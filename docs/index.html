How to measure

Take the three measurements first. Use a flexible tape and keep the subject relaxed and seated. The images below come from the Beam F3 measurement guide.

1 Tragus to tragus (TTT)

Measure straight across the head from left tragus to right tragus.

2 Nasion to inion (NI)

Measure from the nasion over the scalp to the inion.

3 Head circumference (HC)

Measure the head circumference through the FPz-Oz midline plane.

Locate F3

The app calculates X and Y from your measurements and applies the Beam F3 adjustment. Use the steps below to mark the site.

X = 0.1154 × HC   |   Y = 0.2637 × ((TTT + NI) / 2)   |   Yadj = Y + 0.35 cm
  • Measure X along the circumference from the midline toward the left side.
  • From the vertex (Cz), draw a line through the left pre-auricular point.
  • Measure Yadj along that line to locate the F3 point.

Reference map

The EEG reference map helps confirm the F3 region relative to the 10-20 system. Use it alongside the calculated distances for placement.

The app stores every measurement locally, so clinicians can review previous sessions without network access.
